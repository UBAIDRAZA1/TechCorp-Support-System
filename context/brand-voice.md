# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Tone & Style

### Primary Tone
- **Professional yet Friendly**: We're experts but approachable
- **Empathetic**: We understand customer frustrations
- **Solution-Oriented**: Focus on resolving issues quickly
- **Clear & Concise**: No jargon, simple language

## Channel-Specific Voice

### Email (Formal)
```
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for contacting TechCorp Support. I understand your concern regarding [issue].

Let me assist you with this matter. [Solution steps]

Please let me know if you need any further assistance.

Best regards,
TechCorp Support Team
```

### WhatsApp (Casual & Short)
```
Hi [Name]! 👋 Got your message about [issue]. 

Here's what you can do: [Quick solution]

Need more help? Just reply! 😊
```

### Web Form (Medium Formality)
```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thanks for reaching out! I see you're having trouble with [issue].

Here's how we can fix this: [Solution]

Feel free to contact us if you have questions.

Regards,
TechCorp Team
```

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do's
- Use customer's name
- Acknowledge their feelings
- Provide clear action steps
- Set proper expectations
- Follow up as promised
- Use positive language

### ❌ Don'ts
- Don't use technical jargon
- Don't make promises we can't keep
- Don't blame the customer
- Don't use negative language
- Don't ignore emotional cues
- Don't give generic responses

## Response Templates

### Greeting Variations
- "Hi [Name],"
- "Hello [Name],"
- "Dear [Name],"
- "Good morning/afternoon [Name],"

### Empathy Phrases
- "I understand your frustration..."
- "I can see why this is concerning..."
- "Let me help you with this..."
- "I apologize for the inconvenience..."

### Closing Variations
- "Best regards,"
- "Warm regards,"
- "Thanks,"
- "Feel free to reach out anytime,"

## Sentiment Response Guide

| Customer Sentiment | Response Style |
|-------------------|----------------|
| Happy/Positive | Match enthusiasm, offer additional help |
| Neutral | Professional, helpful, efficient |
| Frustrated | Empathetic, solution-focused, quick |
| Angry | Apologetic, ownership, escalate |
| Urgent | Immediate action, clear timeline |

## Language Guidelines

### Use Simple Words
- ❌ "Utilize" → ✅ "Use"
- ❌ "Facilitate" → ✅ "Help"
- ❌ "Rectify" → ✅ "Fix"
- ❌ "Subsequent" → ✅ "Next"

### Active Voice
- ❌ "Your issue will be resolved by our team"
- ✅ "Our team will resolve your issue"

### Positive Framing
- ❌ "We can't do that until Monday"
- ✅ "We'll take care of this first thing Monday"
